Most men need to pay more attention to their health. Compared to women, men are more likely to:Smoke and drinkMake unhealthy or risky choicesPut off regular checkups and medical careThere are also health conditions that only affect men, such as prostate cancer and low testosterone. Many of the major health risks that men face - like colon cancer or heart disease - can be prevented and treated with early diagnosis. Screening tests can find diseases early, when they are easier to treat. It's important to get the screening tests you need.
